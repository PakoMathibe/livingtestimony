VARIOUS MINISTRIES

OBJECTIVES
Worship and Intercession - Create an environment of worship during services and convene prayer meetings and anchor the ministry in prayers.
Youth discipleship and Evangelism - Reach out to communities and internal discipeship Follow through the development of new converts. To build an army of Jesus Christ.
Counselling and Support - To provide spiritual and moral support to families and individuals based on the word of God (we are not phychologists)
Children ministry - To manage and provide children with basic Christian knowledge and maintain order during services.
Admin, Publicity and Events - To promote the ministry internally and externally and manage and coordinate events internally.
Fundraising and donations - To identify opportunities strategies to raise funds
Congregational Care - To provide care and support during and after the service. To welcome and provide information to visitors and new members and also to obtain their needs and information.
Facilities - To maintain and oversee all the facilities including the building and equipment and ensuring that they in working order and secured. 
Protocol - To provide personal and administrative support to the pastors and leadership. To liaise with all the ministries and coordinate information.
