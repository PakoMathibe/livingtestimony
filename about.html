WHO WE ARE

1 Corinthians 1:26-29 New King James Version (NKJV) “For you see your calling, brethren, that not many wise according to the flesh, not many mighty, not many noble, are called. But God has chosen the foolish things of the world to put to shame the wise, and God has chosen the weak things of the world to put to shame the things which are mighty; 28 and the base things of the world and the things which are despised God has chosen, and the things which are not, to bring to nothing the things that are, that no flesh should glory in His presence. ” 

The word of God holds true and The Living Testimony Ministry is a testimony of three individuals who came from humble beginnings through an encounter with God and a burden was laid upon their lives to serve and reach out to the world.

Senior Pastor Pierre and his wife Gladness Mpapele together with Prophetess Josephine Ndlhovu who lived with them for at least 18 years ,are a living testimony after God had touched and transformed their lives. God began to use them mightily in the prophetic ,healing , deliverance and the teaching of the word of God and many people were set free and experienced breakthrough in their lives. The numbers of people visiting their home began to swell and a fully fledged ministry was established since 2013 and we are experiencing glory to glory. Pastor Pierre successfully completed a diploma in ministry in order to be well equipped to sheppered the flock.
